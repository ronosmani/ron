Our Story

Founded in 2023, Premier League Hub began as a passion project between three football enthusiasts who wanted to create the most comprehensive and accessible Premier League statistics platform.

What started as a simple spreadsheet shared among friends has grown into a trusted resource for thousands of fans, fantasy football players, and sports analysts worldwide.

We're proud to remain independent and fan-focused, putting the beautiful game above all else.

Our Mission

"To provide football fans with the most accurate, up-to-date Premier League statistics in an accessible and visually engaging format."
