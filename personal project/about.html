About Premier League Hub

Your ultimate source for Premier League statistics, analysis, and insights

Our Story

From passionate fans to the most comprehensive Premier League stats resource

Founded in 2018 by three football-obsessed friends, Premier League Hub began as a simple spreadsheet shared among our fantasy football group. Frustrated by the lack of accessible, comprehensive statistics, we set out to create something better.

What started as a hobby project has grown into the most trusted Premier League statistics platform, serving over 100,000 fans, journalists, and analysts every month.

We remain independent and fan-focused, putting the beautiful game above all else. Our mission is to make Premier League data accessible to everyone, from casual fans to professional analysts.
